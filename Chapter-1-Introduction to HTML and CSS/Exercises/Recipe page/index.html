Caramelized Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Description

A warm, comforting bowl of oats topped with sweet, buttery apples—perfect for chilly mornings or as a light, dessert-like snack.

Ingredients

  1. For the caramelized apples:
    • 1 medium apple (any variety you like, but Honeycrisp or Fuji work great), peeled (if you like) and cubed.
    • 1 tablespoon butter or coconut oil.
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (or maple syrup or honey).
    • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon.
    • Pinch of salt.
  2. For the oatmeal:
    • ½ cup rolled oats (old-fashioned work best; avoid instant).
    • 1 cup milk or plant-based milk (like oat, almond, or soy).
    • Pinch of salt.
    • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, but delicious).
  3. Optional toppings:
    • Chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, almonds).
    • Extra maple syrup or honey.
    • A scoop of Greek yogurt or coconut yogurt.
    • A dollop of peanut butter or almond butter.
    • Chia seeds or hemp hearts for texture and nutrition.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the caramelized apples:
    • In a medium pan over medium, melt the coconut oil or butter.
    • Add the chopped apples, cinnamon, brown sugar, and a pinch of salt.
    • Stir occasionally for 5-7 minutes until the apples are tender and golden and glossy.→ Tip: Let them sit for one minute between stirring so they'll caramelize properly.
    • Then after being softened and sticky, remove from heat and let stand.
  2. Cook the oats:
    • In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it is warm and just simmering.
    • Add the oats and salt.
    • Turn the heat to low and simmer very gently for 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats are creamy and tender.
    • Add vanilla extract if using.
  3. Assemble your bowl:
    • Spoon the oatmeal into a bowl.
    • Top with caramelized apples, warm.
    • Optional: top with whatever toppings sound good to you (nuts for crunch, nut butter for depth, yogurt for creaminess).
  4. Serve warm and enjoy the cozy vibes.
